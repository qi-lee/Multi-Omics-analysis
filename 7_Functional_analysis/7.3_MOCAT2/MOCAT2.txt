MOCAT2

MOCAT2 (metagenomic analysis toolkit) is a package for analyzing metagenomics datasets.

MOCAT2 is Copyright (c) 2012-2016 Jens Roat Kultima, Luis Pedro Coelho & Shinichi Sunagawa, EMBL and BGI. External software used by the MOCAT2 pipeline are copyright respective authors. 


Reference:
Kultima, J. R., Coelho, L. P., Forslund, K., Huerta-Cepas, J., Li, S. S., Driessen, Bork, P. (2016). MOCAT2: a metagenomic assembly, annotation and profiling framework. Bioinformatics . http://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btw183
